Kitleys

Last year, my friend (Lianne) and I decided to put our heads together to create a business which focused on our values. Half a year later, voila! Kitleys was born!
Our vision is to create products that make it easier to be greener, while also saving people money. Our first product is the 'Make Your Own Oat Milk Kit'.
Inside the kit is all of the equipment and know-how you need to make oat milk at home. Everything that comes in our kit is designed to be repurposed and used for years to come, it's entirely plastic free and sourced in the UK.
